# Cheatsheet
* &lt;panel> - primary container
	* &lt;pr> - panel row
		* &lt;pc> - panel column
			* &lt;image>
			* &lt;button>
			* &lt;text> - text area
				* &lt;text type=‘Form Data’>
					* &lt;prefix> - affix content added in front
					* &lt;suffix> - affix content added at end

#### Escape characters:
& - amp; or #38;  
< - lt; or #60;  
> - gt; or #62;  
' - apos; or #39;
