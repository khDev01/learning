# Cheatsheet
'<panel>' - primary container
  <pr> - panel row
		<pc> - panel column
			<image>
			<button>
			<text> - text area
				<text type=‘Form Data’>
					<prefix> - affix content added in front
					<suffix> - affix content added at end

Escape characters:
& - &amp; or &#38;
< - &lt; or &#60;
> - &gt; or &#62;
' - &apos; or &#39;
